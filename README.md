## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I start the timer?
Tap the green **Start** button. It will turn red and change to **Stop** while running.

### How do I record a lap?
While the timer is running, tap the **Lap** button on the left. Your current lap time will be saved and the lap timer resets.

### How do I reset the timer?
Stop the timer first, then tap the **Reset** button (appears where Lap was).

### How do I change the color theme?
Tap the palette icon (ðŸŽ¨) in the top-right corner of the app to open the theme picker.

### How do I unlock all themes?
The Classic theme is free. To unlock all 8 themes:
1. Tap the palette icon
2. Scroll down to "Unlock All Themes"
3. Tap "Unlock for $2.99"

### How do I restore my purchase?
1. Tap the palette icon
2. Scroll down
3. Tap "Restore Purchase"

### Why can't I see my previous laps after closing the app?
Lap times are stored in memory during your session. When you close the app, lap history is cleared. This is by design to keep the app fast and simple.
