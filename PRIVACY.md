# Privacy Policy for Lap Timer

**Last Updated:** January 27, 2026

## Overview

Lap Timer ("the App") is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.**

The App:
- Does not require an account or login
- Does not collect personal information
- Does not track your location
- Does not access your contacts, photos, or other data
- Does not use analytics or tracking services
- Does not require an internet connection

## Data Storage

All timer data (elapsed time, lap times) is stored locally in your device's memory during use. This data is automatically cleared when you close the app. No data is persisted to permanent storage or transmitted externally.

## In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional in-app purchase to unlock additional color themes. This transaction is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that would collect user data.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date above.
